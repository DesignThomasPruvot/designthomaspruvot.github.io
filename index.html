ABOUT

Thomas Pruvot's creations transcend mere furniture to become a subtle dialogue between art and functionality. Destined for exceptional interiors, his works find their place in design galleries, specialized boutiques, and private collections. They are for those who seek the unique, the rare, and the handmade.

In his hands, gesso, a noble and ancestral material, transforms into vibrant skin, both tactile and visual, evoking sometimes organic softness, sometimes geometric rigor. Each piece tells a story—that of an artisan-poet—where lines and textures dialogue to create a timeless presence.

These works do not merely adorn a space: they invite contemplation, stir emotion, and redefine design itself. They are meant to inhabit places where elegance, creativity, and respect for craftsmanship converge to create a unique and inspiring atmosphere.

"Art is alive because it contains the spirit of the one who created it." — Paolo Coelho

ORGANIC COLLECTION

The Organic collection is born from observing the lines present in our environment. These harmonious organic curves invite the elegance of nature into our interiors. Patterns with soft, rounded forms are created in the studio from pieces of mica, offering a texture evoking leather or animal skin. Each piece in this collection captures the very essence of natural fluidity and exceptional craftsmanship.

COSMATI COLLECTION

In the Cosmati collection emerges a profound geometric dimension. Gesso pieces are cut according to the phi ratio, conferring high symbolic charge inspired by Italian mosaics. The term "Cosmati" refers to Roman artisans of the Middle Ages whose creations still adorn the most beautiful churches in Italy today, recalling the timeless beauty of sacred geometry and divine proportions.

PEAU DE GESSO

The Peau de Gesso collection was created as the logical continuation and synthesis of the first two collections. The effect achieved combines geometric precision with natural organic texture, evoking the phyllotaxis that characterizes the harmonious arrangement of elements in nature. This unique finish evokes snake or pangolin skin, creating a tactile and visual surface of exceptional richness that captures and reflects light in a fascinating way.

LUMINAIRES

These original creations are foremost part of a spiritual and contemplative endeavor. Realized in peace and stillness like medieval Coptic monks, they embody matter become spirit, light revealing the soul of noble materials. Precious materials used include minerals (Brazilian agate, rose quartz and Indian amethyst, salt agate), gesso worked according to ancestral techniques, and solid sipo wood finely hand-carved. Each luminaire is an invitation to meditation, creating ambiences conducive to serenity and inspiration.

Eclat

Translucent Indian salt agate, silver gesso, hand-carved sipo wood
42 × 33 × 16 cm

Luminaire specially designed for bright, luminous ambiances requiring maximum brightness. Created with precise assembly of twelve exceptional salt agate tiles from India, a translucent stone with unique properties. Eclat will illuminate with elegance a place of daily living or a demanding work space.

Intime

Indian rose quartz, crackled rosy gesso with pearlescent nuances, hand-carved sipo wood
42 × 33 × 16 cm

Creation expressing a delicate and more feminine character thanks to its refined assembly of twelve carefully selected gentle rose quartz tiles from India. Visually, it immediately inspires softness, tenderness and benevolence through its soothing pink tones. Intime will naturally find its privileged place in a bedroom or family living room.

Céleste

Indian amethyst, Peau de Gesso, hand-carved sipo wood
42 × 33 × 16 cm

Creation conducive to reading, study and deep meditation, emanating absolute calm and serenity through meticulous assembly of twelve amethyst tiles directly from India. The frame in Peau de Gesso and finely worked sipo wood adds a tactile and visual dimension that enriches the contemplative experience.

Chartres

Mosaic of Brazilian agates, Peau de Gesso, hand-carved sipo wood
42 × 33 × 16 cm

Sacred luminaire created specifically to foster inner peace and spiritual harmony. Its profound inspiration was drawn from Notre-Dame de Chartres cathedral and the timeless magnificence of its original medieval stained glass, absolute masterpieces of Gothic art. The front panel comprises a complex mosaic of natural Brazilian agates in warm and varied tones.

Emanation

Hand-sculpted Indian amethyst, wengé wood frame hand-carved, Peau de Gesso inlaid with copper
52 × 24 × 16 cm

Monumental light stele realized from an exceptional Indian amethyst block, entirely hand-sculpted and pierced with a central light heart. Light diffuses indirectly, tinted with deep purple and warm orange reflections, creating a dreamlike, comforting and deeply intimate atmosphere. Inspired by the refined and mysterious universe of Serge Lutens perfumes, this unique piece evokes an elegant and sophisticated twilight ambiance.

Discover the Chartres and Emanation luminaires integrated into exceptional spaces: intimate lounge, sacred church, and dreamlike ambiances. These creations transcend their function to become living works that transform the atmosphere of the places they inhabit, creating moments of contemplation and wonder.
