ABOUT

Thomas Pruvot's creations transcend mere furniture to engage in a subtle dialogue between art and functionality. Designed for exceptional interiors, his works find their place in design galleries, specialty boutiques, and private collections. They are made for those who seek the unique, the rare, and the handmade.

In his hands, gesso, a noble and ancestral material, transforms into a vibrant skin, both tactile and visual, evoking at times organic softness, at times geometric precision. Each of his pieces tells a story—the story of an artisan-poet—where lines and textures converse to create a timeless presence.

These works do more than adorn a space: they invite contemplation, evoke emotion, and redefine the very notion of design. They are meant to inhabit spaces where elegance, creativity, and respect for artisanal craftsmanship meet in perfect harmony.

"A work of art is alive because it contains the spirit of the one who created it." — Paolo Coelho

COLLECTION ORGANIQUE

The Organic collection is the result of observing the lines found in our environment. These harmonious organic curves invite the elegance of nature into our interiors. The soft, rounded patterns used to cut the gesso pieces were created in the workshop, offering a texture reminiscent of leather or animal skin. Each piece in this collection captures the very essence of natural fluidity and exceptional craftsmanship.

COLLECTION COSMATI

In the Cosmati collection, a profound geometric dimension emerges. The gesso pieces are cut according to the phi ratio, giving them a high symbolic charge inspired by Italian mosaics. The term "Cosmati" refers to the Roman artisans of the Middle Ages whose creations still adorn the most beautiful churches in Italy today, recalling the timeless beauty of sacred geometry and divine proportions.

PEAU DE GESSO

The Peau de gesso collection was created as the logical continuation and synthesis of the first two collections. The resulting effect combines geometric precision with natural organic texture, resonating with phyllotaxis, which characterizes the harmonious arrangement of elements in nature. This unique finish evokes snake or pangolin skin, creating a tactile and visual surface of exceptional richness that captures and reflects light in a fascinating way.

LIGHTING

These original creations are, above all, part of a spiritual and contemplative approach. Created in peace and meditation like medieval copyist monks, they embody matter transformed into spirit, light revealing the soul of noble materials. Precious materials are used: minerals (Brazilian agate, Indian rose quartz and amethyst, salt agate), gesso worked according to ancestral techniques, and solid sipo wood finely hand-gouged. Each lighting fixture is an invitation to meditation, creating atmospheres conducive to serenity and inspiration.

Eclat

Translucent Indian salt agate, silvered gesso, gouged sipo wood
42 × 33 × 16 cm

Lighting fixture specially designed for bright, luminous atmospheres requiring maximum natural luminosity. Created with a precise assembly of twelve exceptional salt agate tiles from India, a translucent stone with unique properties. "Eclat" will elegantly illuminate a daily living space or a demanding work area.

Intime

Indian rose quartz, cracked pink gesso with pearlescent nuances, gouged sipo wood
42 × 33 × 16 cm

Creation expressing a delicate and more feminine character through its refined assembly of twelve carefully selected tiles of soft rose quartz from India. Visually, it immediately inspires softness, tenderness and benevolence through its soothing pink tones. "Intime" will naturally find its place in a bedroom or family living room.

Céleste

Indian amethyst, Peau de gesso, hand-gouged sipo wood
42 × 33 × 16 cm

Creation conducive to reading, study and deep meditation, radiating calm and absolute serenity through a meticulous assembly of twelve amethyst tiles directly from India. The "Peau de gesso" frame and finely worked sipo wood add a tactile and visual dimension that enriches the contemplative experience.

Chartres

Brazilian agate mosaic, Peau de gesso, gouged sipo wood
42 × 33 × 16 cm

Sacred lighting fixture created specifically to foster inner peace and spiritual harmony. Its profound inspiration was drawn from Notre-Dame de Chartres Cathedral and the timeless magnificence of its original medieval stained glass windows, absolute masterpieces of Gothic art. The front panel is composed of a complex mosaic of natural Brazilian agates in warm and varied tones.

Emanation

Hand-carved Indian amethyst, hand-gouged wenge wood frame, Peau de gesso inlaid with copper
52 × 24 × 16 cm

Monumental luminous stele made from an exceptional block of Indian amethyst, entirely hand-carved and pierced with a central heart of light. The light diffuses indirectly, tinted with deep violet and warm orange reflections, creating a dreamlike, comforting and deeply intimate atmosphere. Inspired by the refined and mysterious universe of Serge Lutens perfumes, this unique piece evokes an elegant and sophisticated penumbra.

Discover the Chartres and Emanation lighting fixtures integrated into exceptional spaces: intimate living room, sacred church, and dreamlike atmospheres. These creations transcend their function to become living works that transform the atmosphere of the places they inhabit, creating moments of contemplation and wonder.
